JACK WHITE

Basic Profile

Born: 08/05/1997

Hometown: Traralgon, Victoria, Australia

Number: #41

Position: Forward

ESPN HS Ranking: NR

Season Stats

Role on the Team

As one of two international players on the team, Jack White is a fan favorite. Every time he appears in a game, fans are always cheering for him and are always supportive of him. Fans are always cheering for when White comes in because he isn't in the rotation and mostly comes in during the ending minutes of a game, especially at home. White's strongest assest, though, is his three point shot and this will hopefully help him get quality minutes next year.

Video Spotlight

This video is during Jack White's time playing in Australia and with the youth National Team. See why Duke recruited White as he is a high flyer, an agile forward while hitting the outside shot, and can run the floor well.

Accomplishments & Awards

  • Started all games for the Australian National Team in the 2015 Fiba U19 World Championship
  • Also competed for Australia at the 2014 FIBA U17 World Championship
  • Played in the 2013 FIBA U16 Oceania Championship

  • Finished high school as his team's all-time leader in rebounds and assists and second-leading all-time scorer
