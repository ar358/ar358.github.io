Role on the Team

After Justin Robinson received his full scholarship to play for Duke, Brennan Besser is one of two (the other being freshman Mike Buckmire) walk on players for the team. Because he's a walk on player, he won't see playing time at all unless Duke is blowing out the opponent. Nevertheless, Besser's role on the team is to help the team in practices and in hyping the other guys up while on the bench, as shown in the video below.
