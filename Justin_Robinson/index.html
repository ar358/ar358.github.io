JUSTIN ROBINSON

Basic Profile

Born: 08/05/1997

Hometown: San Antonio, TX

Number: #50

Position: Forward

ESPN HS Ranking: NR

Season Stats

Short Bio

Technically in his junior year at Duke, Justin Robinson redshirted his freshman year and is now considered a redshirt sophomore season for Duke Basketball. Justin Robinson is also known as the guy on the team who is the son of NBA Hall Famer David Robinson, and you may occasionally see David Robinson at some Duke games watching the team play. Also, learn more about how Justin Robinson received his athletic scholarship to play at Duke by watching the video below.

Role on the Team

Another fan favorite, Justin Robinson unfortunately won't see many minutes this season. Although he has appeared in a few contests this year, most of his appearances have been in games that were well secured and in the closing minutes of the second half. Although not seeing any quality minutes this season, Robinson proved in high school that he can be a shot blocker, averaging 4.6 BPG as a senior and 5.6 BPG as a junior, and hopefully he can some minutes his junior or senior year.
