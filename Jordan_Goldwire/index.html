Season Stats

Role on the Team

At 6'2" and 172 pounds, Goldwire will most likely only play the point guard position when Trevon Duval gets some rest. Although Goldwire has gotten some minutes early on in the season, it will be tough for him to get some quality minutes in close games or against tougher opponents as the season progresses. However, if the guards get into foul trouble, look for Goldwire to come in, regardless of who the team is playing.
